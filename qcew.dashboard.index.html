Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages Visualization Project

Description

QCEW program publishes a quarterly count of employment and wages reported by employers covering more than 95 percent of U.S. jobs available at the county.

This dashboard covers data on:

  • Annual Average Employment
  • Annual Average Pay
  • Annual Total Wages
You can also filter data by:
  • Year (2005-2021)
  • State (50 States + Washington D.C.)
  • Industry
This dashboard does not include:
  • Classification by age, sex, race
  • Data on Puerto Rico
  • Employment & Wage data of government workers (federal, state, local)
  • Employment & Wage data of workers whose industry is "Public Administration"
  • Employment & Wage data of workers whose industry is "Unclassified"
